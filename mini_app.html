1. Play Bingo — open the lobby and tap any free number from 1–100. That reserves you a card and costs the stake shown.

2. Start the game — once you've joined, tap Start. Numbers get called automatically and your card marks itself.

3. Win — complete any full row, full column, either diagonal, or all four corners to win the pot.

Points — this is a demo wallet. Use "Add points" from the menu for more play-money at any time.
